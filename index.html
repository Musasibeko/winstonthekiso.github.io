ABOUT ME

Winston Mojalefa Thekiso

Winston Mojalefa Thekiso is a ballpoint pen artist from Mbombela, Mpumalanga, with a diploma in Graphic Design from Technikon Witwatersrand. His work, inspired by social, political, and economic issues in South Africa, delves into human thought, behavior, and philosophy. Known for combining portraiture and landscapes using geometric shapes and flowers, Thekiso's art challenges viewers to derive their own interpretations and offers fresh perspectives on societal stereotypes.
